Long ago, two giants fought in the skies over the world
One was struck down and its blade fell with it, splitting the earth.
To this day, no one has seen the end of the giant sword. Some say it goes on underground forever.
Many venture down the blade anyway, in search of ancient magical artifacts hidden inside.

Setting:
Seems like fantasy on the surface, but has hidden sci-fi elements.
Very clearly inspired by Infinty Blade and Xenoblade Chronicles.
